Gaming

Some of my most favorite games are:

  • Skyrim
  • Assassin's Creed

Skyrim was one of the first games I got into when I first started gaming. My friend told me I should buy it and play it because she liked it. The reason I enjoy playing it is because I enjoy sci-fi and fantasy and Skyrim falls into that category. It is a single player role playing game where the player takes on the role the "dragon born". Of course it includes dragons, but there are other types of characters as well.

Assassin's Creed is also one of the first games I played. The very first Assassin's Creed I played was Revelations. Out of all of the ones I have played my favorite has been Brotherhood. Like Skyrim, Assassin's Creed is a single player role playing game unless playing in multi-player. It takes place in different time periods where the main character is always sent back through the Animus.
