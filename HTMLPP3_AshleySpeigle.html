The City of Lamoni

The Linden Street Coffee House is one of the most popular places in Lamoni when you are looking for some caffeine or simply something to snack on. Within the store you can find many comfortable places to sit down to relax and work on homework or chat with friends. If you ask anyone where they go to wind down it is The Linden Street Coffee House!

The first word I think of when I hear the name "Coliseum" is movies at the movie theater. In Lamoni this is where you go whenever you want to watch the most popular movie of the week. Every movie is always being played on Friday and Saturday every week.

The Coliseum

Lamoni is also home to the college of Graceland University. Graceland was established in 1895 and was only a 2 year college. Now, Graceland allows students to get their bachelors. During the spring and the summer, Graceland is the most beautiful. Everyone who comes to visit during these times would be in awe at how quiet and peaceful it is.

Graceland University

Livestock Auction

Among the streets of Lamoni there is a place to bid on livestock, this place is called the "Livestock Auction". Here citizens and others outside of Lamoni can go to bid on the livestock they want to get. Some of the people who go here either are selling or the ones who want to buy livestock or other animals to bring back to their house or farm.

Lamoni Community Center

The first place you should go when you first arrive in Lamoni is the Lamoni Community Center. When going here you will find it easier to navigate Lamoni once you know all the information about it. Here you are also able to get an idea of what you should do while in Lamoni like where to eat and what stores you should visit before you leave.

My Opinion

The first time I came to visit Lamoni, I really did not know what to think of it because I was not used to such a small town with a just as equally small population. However, this is one of the biggest reasons I came here. I have lived here since September 2017 when I first started college. So far, I have enjoyed how quiet and far from the big city it is. At night I can see the stars and during the spring it is so much better. The same reason I like it is also the same reason I hate it. This is because it is so far away from the nearest store like Wal-Mart and there is barely any restaurants.
